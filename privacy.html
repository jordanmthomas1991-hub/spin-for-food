Privacy Policy

We keep things simple. No accounts, no tracking, no nonsense. Here's exactly what we do (and don't) collect.

Last updated: May 2025

👋 Overview

🎉 Good news: SpinForFood doesn't require an account, doesn't track you, and doesn't sell your data. We're just a food wheel. Spin it and enjoy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information SpinForFood.com ("we", "us", "our") collects when you use our website, how we use it, and what rights you have. We've written this in plain English because legal waffle helps nobody.

By using SpinForFood, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you don't agree, please stop using the site — though we think you'll be fine.

📦 What We Collect

SpinForFood is a client-side web application. This means almost everything happens in your browser, not on our servers. Here's what we actually collect:

  • Nothing you type into the wheel. Your custom food lists, presets, and settings are stored only in your browser's localStorage. We never see them.
  • Basic analytics. We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how people use the site — things like which pages are visited, how long people stay, and what country they're in. This data is anonymised and aggregated.
  • Standard server logs. Our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) may log basic request data such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. We do not control this data and do not use it to identify individuals.

We do not collect: names, email addresses, payment information, precise location data, or any personally identifying information.

🍪 Cookies

SpinForFood uses a small number of cookies and browser storage mechanisms:

  • localStorage — Used to save your theme preference (dark/light mode), your custom food wheel, saved presets, and wheel theme selection. This data never leaves your device.
  • Google Analytics cookies — GA4 uses cookies to distinguish users and sessions for analytics purposes. These are anonymised and do not identify you personally. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

We don't use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing tools.

📊 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use SpinForFood. The data we can see includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • How visitors arrived at the site (e.g. Google search, direct, social media)

This data is processed by Google under their own privacy policy. We have enabled IP anonymisation. We do not use this data to identify individuals, serve personalised ads, or share it with third parties.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

🔗 Shareable Links

SpinForFood lets you generate shareable links that encode your custom food wheel in the URL. When you share a link, the recipient can see the food options you've added — this is intentional and is the whole point of the sharing feature.

We don't store these links on our servers. They exist only as URLs that you choose to share.

🔒 Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's very little to secure. However:

  • SpinForFood is served over HTTPS at all times.
  • Your browser's localStorage data is only accessible to SpinForFood on your device — other websites cannot read it.
  • We don't have a database of user information because we don't collect any.

👶 Children's Privacy

SpinForFood is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from anyone, this is naturally satisfied.

If you believe a child has somehow provided personal information through our site, please contact us and we'll investigate immediately.

🌍 Your Rights (GDPR & UK GDPR)

If you're in the UK or European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since we hold virtually no personal data, most of these rights are satisfied automatically.

For any data held by Google Analytics, you can use Google's opt-out tools or contact us and we will request deletion on your behalf.

For any questions about your rights, contact us at: hello@spinforfood.com

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. We won't notify you individually because we don't have your contact details — which is rather the point.

Continued use of SpinForFood after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
